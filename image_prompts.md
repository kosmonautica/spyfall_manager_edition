This file contains the prompts used for generating the card images.
The prompt consists of of two parts:
- the first part is always the same and ensures that setting, colors and lighting is always the same
- the second part (chapters "scene" and "absurdity" changes per scenario).

ChatGPT Image 2 was used as image generation model.

----

# Part 1: General part of prompt (stays always the same)


## OUTPUT FORMAT
PNG file, landscape orientation, exactly 930 pixels wide and 630 pixels tall.

## ART STYLE
Detailed vintage illustration. The overall tone is very bright and light —
think of a high-key vintage illustration, almost overexposed, like a
sun-drenched 1950s magazine advertisement. The sepia palette is extremely
light: off-white, pale cream, very light warm beige, and soft golden tones.
Deep browns and dark shadows are avoided entirely. The darkest tones in the
image should be no darker than a medium warm tan.
Subtle aged-paper texture, reminiscent of mid-century editorial illustration.
The background, furniture, and props are rendered in a realistic vintage style.
The cat characters themselves are drawn in a cute, comic-like style —
slightly rounded shapes, expressive faces, big eyes, charming and endearing,
like characters from a classic 1950s animated cartoon. The contrast between
the realistic setting and the cute cartoon characters is intentional.

## LIGHTING
Extremely bright, high-key lighting. The scene is flooded with intense warm
white light from all directions — as if shot in a bright studio or a room
with floor-to-ceiling windows on a brilliant sunny day. Shadows are barely
visible, very soft and pale. No dark corners, no moody pools of shadow.
The brightest areas of the image are near-white. The overall impression
is cheerful, light, and airy — like a well-lit 1950s advertising illustration.

## COMPANY SETTING
The scene takes place inside a 1950s computer technology company. The company
develops and operates large mainframe computers. Depending on the scene, the
location may be a computer engineering lab, a management office, a conference
room, a reception area, a server hall, a cafeteria, or any other room typical
of such a company in the 1950s. All rooms reflect the era: heavy wooden
furniture, linoleum floors, drop ceilings with fluorescent lights, large
windows with venetian blinds, and 1950s office decor throughout.

## CHARACTERS
All characters are anthropomorphic cats dressed as 1950s employees of a
technology company — engineers in lab coats with pocket protectors and
slide rules, managers in suits and ties, secretaries in blouses and skirts.
They behave exactly like serious human professionals, completely unaware
of any absurdity. The group of cats must always include both clearly male
and clearly female cat characters in roughly equal numbers. Both genders
are depicted as equally competent, active, and engaged.

## COMPOSITION
The top 20% of the image consists entirely of background only — ceiling,
upper walls, windows, lights, or architectural details. No character, no head,
no hand reaches into the top 20% of the image. All figures are positioned
in the lower 80% of the image.

## TONE
The scene has a slightly surreal, deadpan quality — something is subtly off,
but nobody reacts to it.

## RESTRICTIONS
No text, no speech bubbles. High detail, cinematic framing.
The illustration fills the entire image edge to edge.

## FRAMING
Medium wide shot, slightly low angle, bright even light from above and all sides.

# Scenario 1: Daily Standup

## SCENE
Five cats — a mix of male and female engineers — stand in a tight circle in
the computer engineering lab, holding clipboards with technical diagrams and
notepads, clearly mid-Stand-Up meeting. A hand-drawn Scrum board on the wall
behind them shows sticky notes labeled "TO DO", "IN PROGRESS", "DONE" —
written in neat 1950s typewriter font. Behind them, enormous mainframe computers
fill the room, with blinking lights and spinning tape reels.

## ABSURDITY
One cat is standing on an enormous stack of punch card boxes to reach
the height of the others, completely ignored by everyone.
